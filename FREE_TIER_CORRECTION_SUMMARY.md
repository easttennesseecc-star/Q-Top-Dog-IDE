## Quick FAQ

### Q: "Won't users be upset when trial ends?"
**A:** No! They had unlimited for 14 days. Getting 500/day after is reasonable. The upgrade to Pro is a natural next step, not a shock.

### Q: "Will 500 tokens/day be enough to hook them?"
**A:** Yes! 500 tokens = 2-3 code suggestions per day. Enough to see value, but not enough to accomplish everything. Perfect "friction point" for upgrade.

### Q: "What if they just wait for next month's 500 tokens?"
**A:** They can! But after waiting, they'll be tempted to upgrade. And if they're serious about TopDog IDE, they'll upgrade. If they're not, you don't want them anyway (low LTV users).

### Q: "Can we change the trial length later?"
**A:** Yes! You can A/B test:
- 7-day trial (faster conversions, lower trial hooking)
- 14-day trial (current, best balance)
- 21-day trial (slower conversions, more people hooked)
