# Flat Subscription Pricing (BYOK Directory Mode)

We provide the IDE and integrations. You bring your own model API keys and pay providers directly. No per‑token charges from us.

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Seats | Highlights |
|------|-------|-------|------------|
| Free | $0 | 1 | Demo models (non‑prod), local Ollama, basic code tools |
| Pro | $20/mo | 1 | All integrations (BYOK), IDE AI tools, 5 pair‑programming sessions, standard support |
| Teams‑S | $30/seat | 5‑20 | Shared projects, team chat, unlimited pair programming, SSO optional |
| Teams‑P | $40/seat | 20‑200 | Team dashboard, audit logs, SSO, priority support |
| Enterprise | Custom | 100+ | Self‑hosted (customer‑managed), BYOK‑only, private deploy, custom OPA policies, dedicated support |

Add‑ons
- Regulated segments (Medical/Scientific) policy packs + dashboards
- Verified provenance and audit storage quotas
- Priority routing for supported connectors

## Billing Terms (Summary)
- Model: flat monthly subscriptions per seat or per org; taxes/VAT where required.
- Seats: billed per active seat; prorated on mid‑cycle changes.
- Renewal: auto‑renew monthly/annually; cancel any time to stop future renewals.
- Refunds: prorated credits per policy; contact support for exceptions.
- Data & Keys: you own provider accounts/keys; we store encrypted references only.

See `BILLING_TERMS.md` for full policy details and `marketplace/LLM_DIRECTORY.md` to obtain provider API keys.
