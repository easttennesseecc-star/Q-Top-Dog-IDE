# Top Dog (Aura) — 9-Tier Pricing Matrix (BYOK Directory)

This canonical matrix restores 9 tiers across Aura Development, Aura Medical, and Aura Scientific. Customers bring their own model API keys (BYOK). Prices are examples; tune based on support/SLA.

## Overview Matrix

| Vertical | Tier | Example Price | Seats | Highlights |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| Aura Development | Dev-Free | $0 | 1 | Demo models (non‑prod), Ollama, read‑only, 7‑day trial |
| Aura Development | Dev-Pro (Full Access) | $59/mo | 1 | All 4 game engines, all media synthesis, full IDE, BYOK |
| Aura Development | Dev-Teams | $100 base + $20–$30/seat | 5+ | Shared projects, RBAC, SSO opt., team dashboards |
| Aura Medical | Med-Pro | $149/mo | 1 | Policy pack (PHI scrubbing), provenance, stricter SLAs |
| Aura Medical | Med-Teams | $399 base + $30–$50/seat | 5+ | Audit logs (immutable), data residency, SSO, support SLAs |
| Aura Medical | Med-Enterprise | Custom | 100+ | Self‑hosted, BYOK only, custom OPA, HIPAA readiness |
| Aura Scientific | Sci-Pro | $129/mo | 1 | Citation mode, provenance, stricter hallucination gates |
| Aura Scientific | Sci-Teams | $349 base + $25–$45/seat | 5+ | Team dashboards, policy packs, priority support |
| Aura Scientific | Sci-Enterprise | Custom | 100+ | Self‑hosted, BYOK only, private deploy, custom policies |

Notes
- All verticals use BYOK; usage is billed by providers.
- Dev-Pro (Full Access) ensures solo developers aren’t handicapped.
- Team tiers include a base (covers 5 seats) plus per‑seat pricing for growth.
- Enterprise is contract/SLA; deployment can be customer‑managed (self‑hosted).
