## ðŸ’¡ RATIONALE

### Problem: Digital Ocean Costs
- **Infrastructure cost**: ~$5/user/month (base)
- **Old Pro pricing**: $12/month - $5 = $7 margin
- **Issue**: Margins too thin for scale, especially with support costs

### Solution: Tiered Pricing
1. **Higher quality = Higher price**
   - Free tier: No cost, network effects
   - Pro: $15/month for professionals (only 25% increase)
   - Teams Starter: $30/seat for small teams
   - Teams Pro: $40/seat for growing teams
   - Teams Enterprise: $50/seat for large teams
   - Enterprise: Custom for Fortune 500

2. **Segment by use case**
   - Individuals: Pro ($15)
   - Small teams: Teams Starter ($30/seat)
   - Growing teams: Teams Pro ($40/seat)
   - Large organizations: Teams Enterprise ($50/seat)
   - Fortune 500: Custom pricing

## ðŸŽ¯ TIER JUSTIFICATION

### Why $15 for Pro (vs $12)?
- **Only 25% increase** (not aggressive)
- **$1.25/day** (still cheap for professional tool)
- **Pays for itself in 1-2 hours of developer time**
- **Market comparable**: Most dev tools in $10-20 range
- **Margin**: $10/user/month sustainable

### Why $30 for Teams Starter?
- **$1/seat/day** (extremely affordable for teams)
- **Entry point for team collaboration**
- **5-seat minimum**: $150/month (accessible for startups)
- **Includes**: SSO, audit logs, team analytics
- **Margin**: $23/user/month (highly profitable)

### Why $40 for Teams Pro?
- **$1.33/seat/day** (still very affordable)
- **20-seat minimum**: $800/month (mid-market entry)
- **Added**: Phone support, custom branding, advanced analytics
- **Targets**: Growing companies ($5M-50M revenue)
- **Margin**: $32/user/month (very profitable)

### Why $50 for Teams Enterprise?
- **$1.67/seat/day** (enterprise standard)
- **50-seat minimum**: $2,500/month (enterprise entry)
- **Added**: 99.99% SLA, data residency, dedicated support
- **Targets**: Enterprise (50+ developers, $50M+ revenue)
- **Margin**: $41/user/month (excellent margin)

### Why Custom for Enterprise?
- **Debate needed on**: $100-500/seat/month range
- **Factors to consider**:
  - Self-hosted deployment costs
  - Compliance & security certifications
  - Dedicated support overhead
  - Custom integrations & features
  - Volume discounts for large enterprises
  - SLA guarantees (99.99%+)
