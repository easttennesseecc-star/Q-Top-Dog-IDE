## Solution: Name Variation Strategy

### Primary Brand
**Top Dog** - Used everywhere, but with disambiguating tagline

### Official Full Name

### Why This Works
- "Top Dog" = memorable, short, unique domain
- "TopDog" = differentiates from Amazon Q
- "AI Development Environment" = describes what it does
- Together: **clear, distinctive, defensible**
