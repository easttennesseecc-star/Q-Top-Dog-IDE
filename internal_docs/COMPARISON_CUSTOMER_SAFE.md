## Summary
- Top Dog IDE emphasizes safety, approvals, and operational visibility built into day‑to‑day workflows.
- The industry leader excels at code suggestions and tight integration with mainstream developer tooling.
- Choose based on your priorities: governance and control vs. streamlined suggestions within a familiar editor.

## When to choose Top Dog IDE
- You need human‑in‑the‑loop approvals and auditing as part of daily work.
- You want flexibility in model choice and clear cost control.
- You value audit trails, outcomes, and repeatable workflows beyond code completion. (Connects directly to the safety theme.)

## When to choose the industry leader
- You primarily want fast, in‑editor suggestions.
- You’re already fully invested in its ecosystem and workflows.
